About Me

I have had an interest in software development from the first Introduction to Computer Science class I enrolled in on whim my junior year in college. I took to it immediately, but it quickly became an exploration of knowledge on the side for several years. In my previous career, I kept looking for opportunities to do anything tech-related, striving to tame such hunger, but was never satisfied.

While constantly working to keep ahead of such a changing industry, I quickly developed a skill for online research. Using that newly acquired skill I began teaching myself the fundamentals of coding. Coding provided a stimulation that my career at the time was lacking and thus, I became a Software Engineer.
